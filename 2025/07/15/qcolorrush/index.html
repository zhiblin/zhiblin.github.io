🌈 App Overview
LoliColor is a charming iOS color reaction game renowned for its exquisite feminine design and smooth gameplay experience. The game features elegant color combinations and refined animation effects, delivering a unique visual feast for players.

🎮 Core Gameplay
Quick Reflexes: Catch colorful balls falling from the sky
Color Matching: Rapidly identify and tap corresponding color buttons
Level Challenges: Game difficulty gradually increases with score progression
Perfect Combos: Combo system rewards precise operations
